### Why These Changes:

1. **anchor_year and i_interval attributes**: Provide clear descriptions of what these coordinates represent, making the data self-documenting and easier to understand for users and downstream applications.

3. **calendar attributes**: Stores information about the calendar used for resampling, which is crucial for provenance and traceability in machine learning workflows.

All changes maintain backward compatibility while adding valuable metadata for data provenance and traceability. The implementation follows xarray best practices for coordinate and dataset attributes.
